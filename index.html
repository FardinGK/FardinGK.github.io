About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Civil and Systems Engineering at Johns Hopkins University. My research focuses on Operations Research, Data-Driven Decision Making, and Healthcare Systems Engineering.

I am passionate about leveraging advanced analytical techniques to solve complex problems in healthcare and beyond. My interdisciplinary background in Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, and Systems Engineering allows me to approach challenges from multiple perspectives.
